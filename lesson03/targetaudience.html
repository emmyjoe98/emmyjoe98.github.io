Target Audience

The website will be focused towards people who are constantly on the move and need to stay updated at all times. The website will be focused towards anyone that is old enough to understand what a weather forecast means. This could be anyone from locals just wanting to know when it may start snowing, to people who are planning trips and need to know how to pack.

Personas

Persona 1: On the go Mother
Name: Amelia
Age: 35
Responsibilities: Amelia is a mother of 3 children. Her responsibilities include getting kids ready for the day and taking them to wear they need to be.
Some of these activities include soccer and cross country.
Reason for use: She would use this site to make sure her kids are dressed properly for school and recess and all of the practices and games/meets they may need to be at.

Persona 2: Traveling Business Man
Name: Allen
Age: 45
Responsibilities: Allen travels a lot for work by all different methods of transportation. He has to represent his
firm at large meetings to make sure they get the deals they need. Being on time for those meetings is very important.
Reason for use: He would use this website to ensure that all of his travel is staying on time, and if there are delays he will have enough time to reschedule future events.
